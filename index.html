About Arundhati

Mr. Brahmananda Meher started his 1st generation business & entrepreneurial journey with “Arundhati Jewellers” in the year 2000 from his native district headquarter Balangir.

Arundhati, a leading jewellery brand in Odisha, has a presence in 9 districts with stores in Bhubaneswar, Cuttack, Puri, Berhampur, Rourkela, Sambalpur, Balangir, Bargarh, and Bhawanipatna. Upcoming stores are in Angul and Balasore. The business started in 2000 by Technocrat Er. Brahmananda Meher with just 5 employees in a 200 sq ft store, serving 5,000 customers. Today it spans 2,00,000 sq ft, employs 1,200 people, and serves over 1 crore customers as of 2024. The brand has earned numerous awards and customer appreciation.

Employees in nine different cities are working round the clock to create golden smiles for more than 1 crore satisfied customers. Arundhati Jewellers is known for artistic designs and timeless aesthetics. The phenomenal success of Arundhati has been driven by an unstinting commitment to quality for both product and service, a fact widely recognized across the state.

Arundhati Jewellers is the first jewellery retailer to be awarded in western Odisha with the prestigious ISO 9001:2008 certification. It is a life member of the Gems and Jewellery Federation, and a recipient of the Business Excellence Award along with several other accolades.

More...

Arundhati Group joined the HERO MotoCorp family in 2006 as an authorized dealer with a world-class showroom spread over 25,000 sqft in Balangir. In 2013, the second showroom opened in Bhawanipatna, and in 2021, the third ultra-modern showroom launched in the heart of Bhubaneswar. Arundhati became the first dealer in India to be honored with the BHARAT SHRESTHA award twice — the most prestigious award from HERO MOTO CORP.

Arundhati has been bestowed with more than two dozen awards in India and abroad in its automotive division. Across all divisions, Arundhati Group pledges to provide the best customer experience today and in the future.
